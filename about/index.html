About

I am Indian Entrepreneur trying to solve a few of the challenges involved in Cross-Border E-commerce space through technology innovations. I lead an amazing team at my startup, an online social marketplace which aims to bring the joy of group shopping to Indian buyers.

I also serve as an Advisor in the board of Samagra, an organisation that aims to make toilets “Accessible”, “Affordable” and “Aspirational” for the urban poor through an innovative program.

Education and Professional Accomplishments.

I completed my Btech and Mtech (dual degree course) in microelectronics from IIT Bombay, department of electrical engineering, in 2007. I started my career with IBM Microelectronics, as a semiconductor device modeling engineer. My work with IBM involved Compact modeling of semiconductor devices and 6T SRAM design with 14nm SOI technology.

I joined Cadence design systems as a senior product engineer and subsequently managed a team of product engineers working in the field of Signal Integrity (SI/PI) and IP modeling.

Getting Personal

I am an aboriginal of a small village in central India where there are no roads yet, and I have lived in few of the biggest cities in the world.

I have witnessed the world change from the mid-80s to what it is now—from the perspective of a curious Indian boy who likes to travel. I bring with me the perspective of the extreme ends of the spectrum and the continuum that binds them.

I have traveled:

  • from poverty-ridden rural India to affluent plentiful urban India,
  • from largely illiterate social background to one of the most sought-after grad schools in India,
  • out of a religious society into being an Atheist,
  • from a proverbially successful corporate career to entrepreneurship, and
  • literally on more than 100 thousand km of highways across India in past 5 years.

A hell of a ride it was, and the wheels are still rolling :)
